Our Projects

Our overal aim is to provide shelter, basic necessities and education to the impoverished rural communities of Kien Giang, Vietnam with the collaboration of Chua Buu Son Monastery.

Housing

The basic housing unit costs $1,000. This covers house construction plus bedding, cooking supplies, water jars and food for a single family.
Recently, we have decided to built stronger homes with brick walls that would last longer. The price for such home is approximately $2500.
